Finally Beginning

06.03.2026

Finally! After putting off making this update to my website, today I sat down and decided to just do it. I had been thinking about it a lot over the past couple of weeks, but I had exams, presentations, and so on. It gave me time to think of so many ideas I want to implement here. So many, in fact, that I didn’t know where to start. But yesterday I wrote my last exam, and so here we are! This is my first post on my now-working blog.


Honestly, I don’t exactly know what to write here right now. It feels pretty forced since I don’t want to upload a new version of the website without an actual entry, but I also don’t really know if I have anything to write about right now. But I guess what has been the center of my attention since the start of the year has been my exams. I am slowly approaching the end of my Bachelor’s, so these were somewhat different in form and scope. I had to do much more presenting than writing. Surprisingly, I found that I really enjoyed it. I haven’t really had to present anything since high school, and I thought that was great. After all, I could just write all my exams, which seemed more objective and involved less pressure and less attention. But interestingly, it was quite the opposite — I really missed it.


Another thing about exam season is that one spends much more time at home and is not really running around doing many things, which makes you feel like you aren’t really progressing anywhere. Even though, in truth, the opposite is true. Exams literally do move you forward when you pass them :D. But anyway, when you are at home, you have much more time to think, because my brain seems to enjoy even that more than learning. I try to mostly ignore these thoughts about having to do something right now and achieve this and that. Because objectively, I am moving forward, and I will soon be entering my Master’s and then the job market. So there isn’t really any pressure. But maybe those thoughts are useful as motivation to achieve something and to push myself to find my own path. Because is this narrow path of school, then job, really the true way to happiness for me? That is the question that keeps nagging me. And to be honest, every time I think about it, the answer is different. Is there even a straight answer? As of now, I can’t tell. But hopefully one day I will be able to, and then you will find it here.


So I guess this was very spontaneous, but I hope you enjoyed reading this first post if you made it all the way here. You can find my contacts just below, so feel free to message me about anything. I hope you have a great start to March, and I will try to have one as well. Yours truly,
Martin
